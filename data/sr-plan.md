## 0. RELEASE-BLOCKING SAFETY DEFECT (acted on immediately)

Both audits confirmed: a failed/zero-confidence evaluation renders a **green "Accept" badge with "Accept" preselected** in the decision form (screenshot `14-referral-accept-testla.png`: confidence 0%, all gates not_assessed, "insufficient information"). A schema fallback (`decision:"accept"` constructed on failure, `evaluate.ts:103-113`) presents as advice; agreement is the path of least resistance. **Hotfix dispatched 2026-07-13 (task sr-stub-hotfix), ahead of the rebuild**: server-side presenter suppresses any recommendation for failed/indeterminate runs, decision preselection removed for every outcome, 0.65/0.6 threshold unified, screenshot-14 regression fixture. The durable fix (an `evaluation_runs` outcome store with `completed|indeterminate|failed`, stub-exclusion from all metrics, backfill) lands in rebuild Phase 0/1; a coordinated contract v2 later represents "no advice" without inventing an accept.

## 4b. Captain-supplied business rules (2026-07-13, binding)

1. **Children are IN scope under TDDI** ("Tango Delta Delta Indigo"): Muve takes on children under TDDI; **Ofsted is NOT required — remove all Ofsted machinery/items from the service-referral backlog and rebuild scope.** Children referrals are handled in-scope without an Ofsted regime path; the intake-exceptions lane remains only for genuinely out-of-scope work, not Ofsted-badged children referrals. (Discovery should record the TDDI regulatory basis in the criteria documentation.)
2. **Staffing ratio hours are fixed**: 1:1 = 168 h/week (24×7), 2:1 = 336 h/week, 3:1 = 504 h/week.
3. **Pricing = cost to the LA, flat charge-out rates, NO additional cost factors in pricing windows** (no on-cost/absence/property lines): price = hours × charge-out rate per role. Current rates: HCA £32/h, RMN £65/h. The staffing/price decomposition (P2) shows hours-by-role × rate only; rates live in an admin-editable rate table.
