## 1. Executive summary

P2 turns P1's read-only workspace into durable, replayable human truth: verified fields over an immutable extract, versioned document sets with deliberate re-extraction, per-gate human review against the exact rule text used, flat LA pricing from a versioned rate table, append-only decisions with full version binding and risk-class second approval, decision-bound LA response preparation, and a derived disposition seam. Everything is additive and flag-gated; the legacy surface stays available until the P4 cutover.

**The converged architecture** (both drafts agreed independently; treat as settled):

- **Immutable machine truth + append-only human overlays.** `referrals.extract` is never mutated; corrections live in append-only `referral_field_reviews`; one pure `buildEffectiveRecord()` is consumed by evidence, quality, pricing, readiness, decisions, and responses. Correction values and reasons never enter logs, events, or Sentry (chat-turns privacy pattern, `db/migrations/0006:2-4`).
- **Everything a decision consumed is independently versioned and bound**: source set, extract version, effective-record watermark, criteria snapshot, gate-review revisions, rate-card version, priced lines. Staleness is *derived* from binding mismatches (never a mutated status), and the stale path names exactly what changed.
- **Neutrality invariants**: nothing preselected ever; rationale required on divergence or no-advice; a failed/indeterminate evaluation is a valid manual path (the legacy `recommendation IS NOT NULL` guard in `recordHumanReview`, `db/referrals.ts:938`, is wrong for the workspace); screenshot-14 (`fixture-screenshot-14`, `matrix.ts:290-304`) stays "No recommendation" across every new surface and is excluded from all metrics.
- **P2/P3 fence**: no work items, SLA calendars, outbox sending, capacity, presence, decision-draft persistence, outcome recording, reporting, or cutover (`sr-plan.md:56-58`).

**Two P0/P1 debts land first — they are defects, not new scope** (found by X4, code-verified by C7):

1. **Nursing pricing over-count.** `computeFlatChargeOut` prices "2:1 + nursing" as 336 HCA h + 168 RMN h = 504 h (`src/lib/workspace/flat-pricing.ts:92-116`), violating the binding 336-hour total for 2:1 (`sr-plan.md:63`) and the repo's own nurse-inside-ratio provision policy (`src/lib/commercial/provision.ts:218-222`, `:310-324`). The invariant is **Σ(role hours) = exactly 168/336/504**; the P1 test enshrining 504 h is replaced.
2. **Fixture schema drift.** The matrix builder writes `staffingSignals.ratio`/`nursingCover` and `clinicalNeeds.physicalHealth`/`behavioursOfConcern`, none of which exist on the contract (`matrix.ts:78-87` vs `referral-extract.ts:102-145`) — zod silently strips them. Fixtures are repaired and a parse-survival/unknown-key guard prevents recurrence.

**The five headline resolutions of remaining disagreements** (full table in §5):

- **TDDI/Ofsted (D1):** remove every *live* Ofsted path in P2 — extraction prompt (`prompt.ts:10,15`), routing predicates (`service-line.ts:23-27`, `engine.ts:329-334`, `pipeline.ts:695-703`), active criteria, workspace copy — but keep the `"ofsted"` literal in the frozen contract until the later coordinated contract v2. Decisive new evidence ⊕: `mapReferralRow` runs `parseReferralExtract` on **every row read** (`db/referrals.ts:218`) against the strict enum (`referral-extract.ts:83`); dropping the literal makes every historical ofsted-tagged row unreadable. A model-emitted `"ofsted"` after the prompt fix routes to a deterministic `regime_retriage_required` review reason — it is never evaluated against a retired ruleset and never silently relabelled.
- **Commercial (D4–D6):** one canonical flat engine with the corrected arithmetic and fail-closed versioned rates; pricing is *derived* from the effective record + rate card, and the decision binds the priced snapshot — no working-plan-revision store unless the study proves reviewers need to price against something other than the record (Q7). Legacy pricing windows are brought into §4b compliance by a minimal legacy-surface PR (panel read-only, forbidden lines hidden, chat UI flag-disabled, `rateVsCost` overlay stopped for new snapshots) rather than replacing the active domain model mid-rebuild.
- **Evidence model (D9):** X4's immutable source-set graph (documents + set headers with candidate/active/superseded + membership rows + extract versions) wins over C7's `doc_set_version`-only model — point-in-time set reconstruction requires immutable membership. Field reviews land **after** the versioning foundation (X4's ordering correction of C7).
- **Criteria replay + gate reviews (D12–D13):** immutable criteria snapshot + gate→rule grounding sidecar per evaluation run; standalone append-only `referral_gate_reviews` for resumable multi-session review. C7's currency-chip-only and contract-field proposals are withdrawn.
- **Study gating (D19):** foundations build now; UI PRs may **merge dark** with stated defaults but **cohort enablement requires** the completed P1 moderated study plus a recorded study-impact note per UI PR. No second-approval bypass of any kind exists (D15).
